Instructions

How to delete your account

  1. Send an email to henglihov88@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete Account Request.
  3. Include your account email, username, or other identifying details used in the app.
  4. Wait for confirmation after verification is completed.

Data that may be deleted

  • Your account profile and associated account information.
  • App-related user data stored for your account.
  • Other removable records associated with your account, where applicable.

Data that may be retained

  • Records required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution purposes.
  • Limited technical logs retained for a necessary period.
